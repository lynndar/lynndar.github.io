Services Included:

Free Contiental Breakfast for all hotel quest Breakfast is served from 6am to 9am each day.

A room full of up to date exercise equipment for all your needs.

We have a room designated for all our up to date computers to aide you in your genealogy research while you're staying with us.

Indoor heated pool for all your relaxing needs.

We have family history consultants on site and also family ordinance card service.

We have a large laundry room for our guest to use. There is a small fee for purchase of laundry soap.

For your convenience, we have a suttle bus that will take you to and from the temple at no extra cost.

We have a large changing room for all those who have traveled a long way and would like to freshen up before going to the temple. This is a free service for all those attending the temple.

For a small fee, we offer daycare for those who have traveled with children and would like to spend time at the temple.For those local, we have a list of youth willing to watch yoour children at your home.
